Privacy Policy for ₹wise

Effective Date: April 2026

Thank you for using ₹wise. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

1. Information We Collect

  • Mobile Number: Used for account authentication and subscription access.
  • SMS Data (Transaction Alerts Only): We request access to SMS messages only to identify and display financial transaction alerts (such as UPI or banking notifications) required for the core functionality of the app.
  • Usage Data: Basic, anonymized data to improve app performance and stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify and display transaction alerts within the app dashboard.
  • To help users track spending patterns and history.
  • To authenticate users and manage subscription plans.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All financial transaction data is processed and stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload, store, or sync your SMS data or financial information to external servers.
  • We apply reasonable security practices to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal or financial data with third parties.

5. Permissions Usage

  • SMS permission is used strictly to access transaction-related messages required for core app functionality.
  • We do not read or process personal messages unrelated to financial transactions.
  • We request only the minimum necessary access required for the app to function.

6. Subscription Information

  • The app provides a 3-day free trial and a 1-year premium subscription plan.
  • Subscription status is securely managed.

7. User Consent

We request user consent before accessing SMS data. Users can revoke permissions at any time through device settings.

8. User Rights

Users can clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored information. For account-related queries, users can contact us.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13.
